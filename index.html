How often should I inspect my roof?

It is recommended to inspect your roof at least twice a year, in the spring and fall. Regular inspections help identify potential issues early, ensuring your roof remains in good condition and extends its lifespan.

What are the common signs of roof damage?

Common signs of roof damage include missing or broken shingles, water stains on ceilings or walls, sagging areas, and visible leaks. Regular inspections can help identify these issues early.

What is the lifespan of a typical roof?

The lifespan of a typical roof depends on the material used. Asphalt shingles last 20-30 years, metal roofs can last 40-70 years, and slate or tile roofs can last over 100 years with proper maintenance.
